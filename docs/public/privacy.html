Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-04-18 · Last updated: 2026-04-18

LingxY ("the App") is a personal-productivity desktop application. This policy describes what data the App collects, where it is stored, and how it is used when you grant the App access to third-party accounts via OAuth (e.g. Google, Microsoft).

1. What we collect

  • Account identity — your email address, display name and OAuth refresh/access tokens issued by the provider you authorize (e.g. Google, Microsoft).
  • Content you explicitly share with LingxY — the text, file, screenshot, or voice recording you submit as a task, plus any web-page selection you send via the browser extension.
  • Task history & settings — the commands you run and your local preferences (providers, shortcuts, wake-word enrollment).

2. Where it is stored

All of the above is stored locally on your own device, under %LOCALAPPDATA%\UCA\ and %APPDATA%\UCA\ on Windows (this path may be updated in future versions after a compatibility migration). Tokens are encrypted using OS-provided APIs where available. LingxY does not operate its own cloud database.

3. When we send data to third parties

LingxY forwards content to third-party APIs only when you explicitly request an action that requires it. Examples:

  • When you run an AI task, the prompt and any attached context are sent to the AI model provider you have configured (e.g. OpenAI, DeepSeek, Anthropic).
  • When you ask LingxY to read or send email, calendar, or files, the relevant request is sent to the OAuth provider (e.g. Google APIs, Microsoft Graph) using the scopes you granted on the consent screen.
  • When you capture webpage context via the browser extension, the capture payload is delivered to the LingxY runtime on your local machine only.

We do not sell, share, or otherwise transfer your data to parties other than those you have explicitly authorized by configuring a provider or granting an OAuth scope.

4. Google user data (Google API Services User Data Policy)

LingxY's use and transfer of information received from Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements. Specifically:

  • We use Google user data only to provide or improve user-facing features within LingxY.
  • We do not transfer Google user data to third parties except as required by law, or with your explicit consent.
  • We do not use Google user data for advertising.
  • We do not allow humans to read Google user data unless we have your affirmative consent or it is required for security/debugging by our engineers under appropriate confidentiality safeguards.

5. Retention and deletion

Because your data lives on your device, uninstalling LingxY and deleting the UCA folders under %LOCALAPPDATA% and %APPDATA% removes all locally stored data. To revoke LingxY's access to a third-party account, use the provider's account security page (e.g. Google Account → Security → Third-party access).

6. Children

LingxY is not directed to individuals under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

7. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the App evolves. The effective date above reflects the most recent revision.
